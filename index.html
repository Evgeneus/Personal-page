Hello! I am a PhD at the University of Trento, Italy. In my PhD, I work on optimisation a finite pool classification problem in identifying items that pass a set of filters (e.g., in systematic literature reviews, where candidate documents are screened based on a set of criteria to asses whether they are in scope for the review). To this end, I work on crowdsourcing and hybrid crowd-machine learning algorithms that optimise the classificatoin process, taking into account the accuracy vs money trade-off. I explore active learning and reinforcement learning to determine how artificial intelligence and crowdsourcing can benefit from each other.

Research Interests:

Deep Learning Deep Metric Learning Graph Neural Networks
Crowd-AI Algorithms Statistical Inference Natural Language Processing
